Atrophy of Thenar Muscle

Wasting of the hand thenar muscle of in a 18 year old girl having cervical ribs.

Thenar atrophy can be caused by a variety of disorders, including carpal tunnel syndrome, motor neuron disease, Hirayama disease, multifocal motor neuropathy, Lewis-Samner syndrome, Hodgkin syndrome, cervicalspondylotic myelopathy & syringomyelia.
